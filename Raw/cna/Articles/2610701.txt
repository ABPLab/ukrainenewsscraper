FILE PHOTO: The Intel Corporation logo is seen on a display in a store in Manhattan, New York City, US, Nov 24, 2021. (Photo: Reuters/Andrew Kelly)
Intel Corp is suspending all business operations in Russia, effective immediately, the US chipmaker said on Tuesday (Apr 5), adding that it has implemented business continuity measures to minimise disruption to its global operations.
"Intel continues to join the global community in condemning Russia's war against Ukraine and calling for a swift return to peace," it added.
